About me

Born in 1989, I was raised in the small German state called Saarland. After finishing my dual vocational training as a graphic designer, I traveled through New Zealand, Australia and the U.S. for a 3 years. Afterward, I briefly studied in Stockholm, Sweden followed by working as a desktop publisher on cruise ships getting to see the world. Eventually, I settled in the beautiful city of Hamburg to work for the same cruise ship company. Now I am eager to start a new adventure by deep diving into the exciting world of web development, getting to know new skills and people.
